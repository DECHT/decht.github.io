Vožyk

Jan 26, 2020 magazine

Vožyk (Belarusian: Во́жык) is a Belarusian humour magazine offering satire, cartoons, caricature and humorous essays to its readers. Vožyk stands for “hedgehog” in Belarusian. It is published monthly in Minsk, Belarus, in the Belarusian language.
